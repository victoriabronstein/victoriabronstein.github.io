SHINOLA

By Victoria Bronstein

Detroit is an up and coming city due to the innumerable number of small businesses that are in the city. My favorite is Shinola, which is known for its bicycles, watches, and clothing. I personally own a watch and have gotten so much use out of it. If you get a change, I definitely recommend checking this place out. If you are into business, Shinola also offers notepads and pens that are classy and come in all different colors.
