CANFIELD

By Victoria Bronstein

A major historical street and sight in Detroit is Canfield. Located close to Shinola, Canfield is home to beautiful and historic houses. Doctors and lawyers in the city would choose these houses to live in due to their prime location and the beauty of the houses. The homes in Canfield are so unique due to their unique architecture, known as Queen Anne Architecture.
