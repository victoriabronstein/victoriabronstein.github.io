COFFEE

By Victoria Bronstein

A city is not a real city without some great coffee, and detroit is second to none when it comes to coffee. Great Lakes coffee is a modern styled coffee shop with reclaimed woods that adds a rustic feel to the interior. Great Lakes Coffee also offers amazing pour over coffee, and is a great place to study. Another great coffee shop is Astro Coffee which is more than just for coffee lovers, but also a great place for those looking for a good sandwich and place to study.
