THE DETROIT INSTITUTE OF ART

By Victoria Bronstein

Detroit is full of fun museums that are great for families to go to on a weekend or rainy day. My personal favorite is the Detroit Institute of Art. The Museums is full of amazing art and delicious food. The best time to go is with the family is on Family Sundays, but if you are looking to go with a date, I would recommend going to Fridays for the special, “Friday Night Live” celebration. When you are there, don’t miss out on Rivera Court, the museum’s prime attraction.
